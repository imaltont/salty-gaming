About us

History

It was a cold, harsh winter night at an unspecified place and time when founder and de facto leader Arnt Bjornson realized only he could pull e-sports out of the sorry state it found itself in.
He envisioned a future where e-sports athletes were celebrated like the heroes they were.
In order to achieve this he knew he first had to make an e-sports organization to rule all e-sports organizations. For this he needed a name that encompassed all that is gaming, Salty Gaming was born.
