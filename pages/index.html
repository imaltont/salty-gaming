EN TARO ADUN! The New Starcraft II Expansion In Stores Now!

The new expansion, Legacy of the Void, features an epic single-player story, all-new cooperative modes, and intense competitive multiplayer gameplay.

The protoss armada prepares for battle, and the countdown to reclaim Aiur has begun! In Legacy of the Void, players will guide protoss legends Artanis and Zeratul as they fight to unite the protoss race, take back their homeworld, and vanquish the ancient evil that threatens the universe.

As you know, Salty Gaming has two of the most esteemed people in the Starcraft II pro scene and as such we are very excited for what is to come. In order to celebrate this great milestone in pro-gaming, Salty Gaming will hold an invitational tournament with HUGE cash prizes, stay tuned for more information!

Espen "Damz" Dammen Joins Salty Gaming

The "Salty Gaming" organization is proud to announce that Damz is to join its venerated and highly relevant hearthstone roster. He is known among other things for achieving rank 80 on the ladder in season 13, a testament to his high-level mastery of RNGesus' teachings.

The founder and de facto leader of Team Salty Gaming, Arnt Bjornson, had this to say about this glorious addition: "have spent a tremendous amount of time, effort and money to finalize this deal, and I am sure it will pay off in the future."

When asked about his thoughts on joining the organization Damz responded:"think this is fantastic. I believe I'll be able to reach new heights with the support of this oraganiztion." He then added, "Expect me at Blizzcon next year!"

We wish him the best of luck in reaching his goals, and may RNGesus be forever in his favour.
